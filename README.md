Marketing Campaign Performance Dashboard using Power BI

Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end Marketing Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI.
It analyzes campaign performance across multiple dimensions such as platform, industry, campaign and time.

The goal of this dashboard is to help marketing teams:

* Track key performance metrics
* Identify top-performing channels and campaigns
* Optimize ad spend based on ROAS


Key Features

* Interactive and dynamic dashboard
* Multi-page navigation for deep-dive analysis
* KPI tracking for marketing performance
* Clean and modern UI design


KPIs Tracked

* Conversions
* Revenue
* ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)
* Profit
* MoM%


Dashboard Pages

1️⃣ Platform Analysis

* Performance comparison across platforms (Google, Meta, etc.)
* Identify which platform drives highest revenue and ROAS

2️⃣ Industry Analysis

* Analyze performance by industry segment
* Helps in identifying high-converting industries

3️⃣ Campaign Analysis

* Deep dive into campaign types and performance
* Compare efficiency across different campaign strategies

4️⃣ Monthly Analysis

* Time-based trend analysis
* Track growth in revenue, conversions, and ROAS over months

Key Insights 

* With a profit margin of 79.5% and ROAS 4.88, we can conclude that the campaign is successful
* TikTok Ads deliver the highest ROAS and profit, while Google Ads drive the highest conversions and revenue
* SaaS and Edtech industries contributed highest revenue, profit and ROAS
* Search campaigns are the primary driver of revenue, indicating strong high-intent user engagement and effective demand capture.

Recommendations

Scale high-performing campaigns:
With a 79.5% profit margin and ROAS of 4.88, gradually increase budget allocation (10–20%) while closely monitoring performance to avoid diminishing returns.
Prioritize efficient campaign scaling:
Focus on top-performing campaigns and ad sets instead of scaling all campaigns uniformly to maintain overall efficiency.
Leverage platform strengths:
Increase investment in TikTok Ads to capitalize on its high profitability and ROAS, while continuing to utilize Google Ads for driving conversions and revenue.
Optimize underperforming channels (Meta Ads):
Improve performance by refining audience targeting, reducing overlap, and testing new creatives and formats. Incorporate lookalike audiences to enhance reach and engagement.
Enhance industry-specific strategy:
For underperforming industries like FinTech and Healthcare, develop tailored messaging, improve landing page experience (UI/UX), and reassess audience targeting to boost conversion rates.
